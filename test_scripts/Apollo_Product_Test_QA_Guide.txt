Q01  How many total inpatient encounters were recorded for Cardiology at FAC-001 this year?
DB: apollo_analytics · PostgreSQL  ·  Table(s): encounter_summaries
Expected Answer: Returns SUM(total_encounters) for INPATIENT encounters filtered to FAC-001 + Cardiology for current year. Expected: ~1,200–1,500 encounters.
SELECT
    SUM(total_encounters)   AS total_inpatient_encounters,
    SUM(total_admissions)   AS total_admissions,
    SUM(total_discharges)   AS total_discharges
FROM encounter_summaries
WHERE facility_id    = 'FAC-001'
  AND department_name = 'Cardiology'
  AND encounter_type  = 'INPATIENT'
  AND report_month   >= DATE_TRUNC('year', CURRENT_DATE)
LIMIT 1000;
Policy Gate: CLIN-001: FAC-001 row filter + Cardiology dept. Aggregate only — no PHI. HIPAA-001 max_rows=1000.


Q02  What is the average length of stay for cardiac patients across all Apollo facilities?

Q03  Which departments at FAC-001 had a readmission rate above 5% last quarter?

SELECT
    department_name,
    ROUND(AVG(readmission_rate),2) AS avg_readmission_pct,
